Frequently Asked Questions

Insights for serious entrepreneurs and investors.

Is it the right time to invest in a franchise or existing business?

Absolutely. With India's rapidly growing consumer base, acquiring an established business or proven franchise model drastically reduces the time-to-market and failure risk compared to building a startup from scratch. We project a steady 15-20% YoY appreciation for premium acquisitions.

How is buying through you different from direct negotiation?

As Vice President at BusinessEx, I secure bulk institutional allotments and have access to off-market deals. This means you get access to priority unit selection and pre-launch pricing that is simply unavailable to direct walk-in clients. My advisory is completely unbiased across multiple brands.

Do you also assist with real estate investments?

Yes. As an Independent Real Estate Consultant, I provide strategic advisory on securing the right commercial and residential properties to complete your asset portfolio. Whether you need retail space for your new franchise or high-yield luxury real estate, my team manages the entire process.

Do you assist with business valuation and due diligence?

Yes. A business is a lifecycle investment. My team handles end-to-end management, including strict financial audits, legal structuring, securing funding, and eventually managing secondary market exit strategies when you want to book profits.
